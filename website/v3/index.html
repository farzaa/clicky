✿ Common questions

Asked a lot. Answered once.

How does Sensei actually see my screen? +

When you hold ⌃ + ⌥ and ask a question, she takes a single screenshot of whatever's on screen right then. That snapshot goes along with your question so she has context — it isn't recorded, saved, or sent anywhere else. It lives only for the moment she answers, then disappears.

Does it work with every app? +

Yes. Sensei works at the screen layer, not the app layer, so there's nothing to integrate. Browser tab, Excel, Figma, that weird internal tool nobody documented — if you can see it, she can guide you through it.

What's a credit? +

One credit = one guidance turn. You ask, she answers (and points, or teleports, or both). New accounts start with 15 free credits. After that, top up whenever — packs start at $5, and credits never expire.

Do I need a subscription? +

Nope. Everything is pay-as-you-go at launch. Buy credits when you're running low, unlock extra senseis when you want them, that's it.

Which Macs does it run on? +

macOS 14.2 or newer, Apple Silicon or Intel. Sensei lives in your menu bar, never the dock — she's asleep until you hold the hotkey.

What data is stored? +

The bare minimum. Your question and the one-off screenshot go to the AI only to produce an answer; nothing about your screen is retained after the reply. No training, no logging, no background capture.

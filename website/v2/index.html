✿ Three small miracles

What she does, exactly.

三つのふしぎ — three things, nothing more.

壹 One

She points at things.

ポインティング

Ask a question about whatever's on your screen and she draws a little circle around the exact thing you meant. No explaining, no coordinates, no "the third button from the right."

↳ works across every monitor you own.

貳 Two

You talk, she listens.

お話しモード

Hold ⌃ + ⌥, say anything in plain voice, let go. She hears you, thinks for a beat, and replies out loud like a friend who's right there.

↳ no keyword, no "hey siri."

参 Three

She teleports.

ポータル展開

When the thing you asked about is on a different screen, she opens a portal and just goes there. Because scrolling through your own monitors is silly, and anime teachers have powers.

↳ ok it's just a very fast window animation. but still.

✿ From shortcut to portal

Three taps. One moment.

  1. 01

    Hold the key.

    Press ⌃ + ⌥ anywhere on your Mac. Sensei wakes up beside your cursor.

  2. 02

    Ask the thing.

    Talk like you would to a friend. "Where's the export option?" "Why is this red?" "Help me write this email."

  3. 03

    Watch her go.

    She talks back, draws on your screen, and — when the answer is somewhere else — opens a portal and takes you there.

✿ Runs in the menu bar. Lightweight. Never stores your screen. 画面を見るだけ、残さない。
